An Ideal Candidate for Your Intern Position

Advancing into the last year of my Master's in Computer Science, specializing in Data Science/Engineering, I'm deeply engaged with a challenging set of courses, building on my double Bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science.

The afternoons are dedicated to cracking machine learning algorithms and implementing them using Python's libraries. No matter how tough the day has been, I make it a point to solve a problem on LeetCode to keep my coding skills sharp and to stay interview-ready.

It's this intersection of strategy, creativity, and teamwork that I love bringing to every team effort, both on and off the field.

Every week, I am actively shaping my future—a future where I can take complex data and make it meaningful and useful.

As I look for an end-of-study internship, I'm eager to find a place to apply what I've learned and to continue my journey of never-ending learning. If you need someone with a solid comprehension of database theory and practical coding skills, who works well with others, I’d be happy to talk.
